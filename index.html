Brook gives me so much that I wanted to give something back to her and so I trained to become a Reiki Practitioner - the energy work I do with Brook and other dogs helps them if they're anxious or suffering from a whole host of other behavioural problems. I can honestly say that having had Reiki Brook is calmer and more settled than ever before!

I love doing this work and I want to be able to bring my expertise and to you and your family. If you think I can help you with your dog then please do get in touch. I'm fully insured and DBS checked.

What is animal reiki?


Reiki is an ancient therapy that is done by channelling energy in order to heal physical, mental and emotional ailments for dogs. It can be done in person, or from a distance by an Animal Reiki Practitioner.

What can I expect from a session?


I'll want to come and meet you and your dog beforehand to get a sense of what's going on for them and to hear your perspective. After the consultation and providing I can help you I usually suggest booking in 4 sessions minimum so we can work through the issues over time and so that you can really see them progress.
